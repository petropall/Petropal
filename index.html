Our Story

Fuel Delivery, Reimagined.

PetroPal was born out of a frustrating midnight experience — our founder ran dry on a remote highway with no pump in sight. He spent hours waiting for help and thought: "There has to be a better way."

Since 2021, we've been building the infrastructure to make on-demand fuel delivery a reality across India and beyond. From solo travelers to large corporate fleets — we're here to keep the world moving.

SpeedWe obsess over delivery time. Our average is under 30 minutes.
SustainabilityOur routing algorithm minimizes fuel waste in our own deliveries.
TrustAll drivers are background-verified and certified fuel handlers.
